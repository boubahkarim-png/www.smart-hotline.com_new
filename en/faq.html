Frequently Asked Questions

Find quick answers to your questions about our telephony services.

How does the free trial work?

The 2-week free trial allows you to test our services without commitment. You get 20h/week at $19/hour. At the end, you can choose to continue or stop with no fees.

What are your business hours?

We are available Monday to Friday from 9am to 6pm (Eastern Time). For emergencies, our 24/7 support is always reachable.

Can I change my plan at any time?

Yes, you can adapt your plan at any time with 7 days notice. Changes take effect at the beginning of the following month.

What payment methods do you accept?

We accept payments by credit card, bank transfer and monthly direct debit. Payments are secure and GDPR compliant.

How do you ensure data confidentiality?

We strictly respect GDPR. All your data is encrypted and stored on secure servers. We never share your information with third parties without your consent.

Do you offer discounts for long commitments?

Yes, we offer a 10% discount for any 3-month or longer commitment. Contact us to learn more about our special offers.

What services do you offer exactly?

We offer 4 main services: call reception (never miss a call), outbound calls (prospecting and appointment setting), customer support (24/7 assistance) and CRM & Lists (contact management and prospecting).

How does service setup work?

After registration, we schedule a configuration call. We set up your service in 24-48h, train our team on your business, and you're operational.

Can I get a custom phone number?

Yes, we offer local, national and international numbers. You can also keep your existing number or choose a new memorable one.

What is the minimum commitment duration?

Our contracts have no minimum commitment. You can cancel at any time with 30 days notice. We believe our service quality will naturally retain you.
